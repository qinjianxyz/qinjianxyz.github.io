Research agenda

What the lab is trying to learn.

Simulation

Can an agent produce engineering work a reviewer can actually audit?

The target is not a plausible answer. It is a traceable path from geometry and assumptions to solver evidence, limitations, and a reviewable decision.

Robotics

How should software intelligence cross the boundary into real hardware?

Through staged capability, explicit safety and failure states, simulation-to-hardware lineage, and claims small enough to verify.

Execution

How can one small lab sustain work that normally needs a larger organization?

By turning planning, implementation, testing, research, memory, and review into a disciplined system rather than a collection of prompts.
