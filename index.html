About
Welcome! I am a Software and Mechatronics Engineer located in California. I am passionate about problem solving and developing innovative technology. Feel free to reach out.

For Software Engineering, I have extensive experience in design patterns, algorithms, and abilities to work across platforms from web, mobile, desktop development to databases. In my projects, I have used Java, Python, C++, JavaScript, React.js, React Native, Node.js, Electron.

For Mechatronics Engineering, I have built autonomous vehicle, pendulum, various robots for competiton involving skills including computer vision, 3D modeling, simulation, manufacturing, etc. I am expert in engineering software such as Solidworks, AutoCAD, MATLAB, Inventor.
