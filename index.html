About
Welcome. I am a software and mechancial engineer located in California. I am deeply passionate about problem solving and developing innovative technology.
For software engineering, I have extensive experience in design patterns, algorithms, abilities to work across platforms from web, mobile, desktop development to databases. In my projects, I have used Java, Python, C++, JavaScript, React.js, React Native, Node.js, Electron, etc.
For mechanical engineering, I have built autonomous vehicle, pendulum, various robots for competiton involving skills including computer vision, 3D modeling, simulation, manufacturing, etc. I am expert in engineering software such as Solidworks, AutoCAD, MATLAB, Inventor, just to name a few. In fact, my passion in software engineering originated from engineering software.
Besides work, I love fitness, food, and quality time with family. Feel free to reach out anytime if you have any questions, collaboration requests, or work opportunies.
